Elevating User Engagement: The Story Behind the Xlance Collective Website Redesign


Project Overview

Xlance Collective redesigned their website for better usability. We aimed for a clean, minimalist interface to attract forward-thinking clients. We emphasized user-centered design, usability testing, and WordPress development to create an innovative and intuitive web experience.

The goal is to enhance the usability and user experience of the website, ensuring that Xlance Collective's expertise and services are presented in a more intuitive and engaging manner to potential clients.


My Role

Junior UX Designer, Junior Wordpress Developer

Duration

September, 2023 - October, 2023 (1 month)

Tools Used

  • Figma
  • Trello
  • Google Meet

Team

  • 1 UX Designer/Wordpress developer
  • 1 Project Manager
  • 1 Product Owner

Timeline

  • Overall 4 weeks
  • Research & Design 3 weeks
  • Development & testing 1 week

Background

Xlance Collective, a local start-up software company in Myanmar, founded with a vision to build a future by empowering clients, has established itself as a trusted technology partner known for its expertise, creativity, and commitment to delivering exceptional software solutions in web and mobile development.


I was the sole Designer at Xlance Collective


When I joined Xlance Collective, I was the sole designer among a team of over 10 developers. My initial project at Xlance involved redesigning the company's existing website to address usability concerns and enhance the overall user experience.

Understanding the problem

The current website for Xlance Collective lacks usability, particularly in the area related to user contact and interaction. Users find it challenging to utilize the contact form effectively. This has resulted in a suboptimal user experience and reduced engagement levels on the website. Additionally, based on secondary research findings, the existing contact form is overly complex and slow down user interaction.

My research findings helps me:

  • Understand the user goals and needs
  • Uncover pain points within the existing user journey

Gathering insights

Based on the secondary research and informal discussions with colleagues and friends, regarding the issues with Xlance Collective's current website and contact area usability,

Here are some insights derived from the research:

  • users struggle to locate the contact information
  • current contact form is overly complex and slow down the user interaction
  • usability issues with the contact area increase drop-off rates
  • need to create a more intuitive, accessible, and inviting contact interface

Personas

To understand our users better, we created two personas for each user segment based on secondary research and surveys. These personas guided us throughout the project, ensuring a user-centric approach.

Tech-savvy user persona


Traditional user persona

User Journey Map

Based on our research, we recognized that there were 2 key user types that our product tried to solve problems for. We decided to focus on Tech-savvy user persona since their need was greatest for these kind of digital services.

User flow

After creating the User Journey Map, I crafted a User Flow to enhance the contact experience on Xlance Collective's website. This flow details a step-by-step process aimed at enabling visitors to easily locate contact information and interact with a user-friendly form.

Information architecture

To organize the content and structure of the "Xlance Collective" website, I created the Information Architecture. It ensures that users can easily access the navigation, our services, our work portfolio, our team and contact area without any confusion.

Wireframe

I used Figma to transform my initial sketches into low-fidelity wireframes.

Design

For the website, we wanted to create a minimalist and clean UI that conveyed trustworthiness and progression for future-oriented individuals.

Home section

For our website's home section, we aimed for a minimalist, trustworthy design that conveys Xlance Collective's vision. The layout emphasizes simplicity for easy navigation and engagement with our services.

Service section

In our services section, we've designed a clean user interface that conveys trust and innovation, appealing to forward-thinking individuals. Our UI presents a diverse range of services offered by Xlance Collective with clarity and professionalism, reflecting our commitment to web and mobile solutions.

Our works section

This section features a dynamic carousel slider showcasing a selection of our impactful projects and successful collaborations. Our goal is to highlight our expertise and innovation through compelling visuals, demonstrating our capability to deliver impactful web and mobile solutions. By showcasing our past work in a visually engaging format, we aim to instill confidence in visitors and convey our commitment to excellence in every project we undertake.

Developmet process section

In this section, our aim was to ensure that all visitors, whether they are tech-savvy or have limited technological knowledge, can grasp our approach to web and mobile development. We emphasized key stages including planning, design, development, testing, and deployment. We also added a GIF, illustrating the Software Development Life Cycle (SDLC) to enhance the visual representation of our development process.

Technology Stack & Our Team Section

There's a "Technology Stack" section before “Our Team” section. We wanted to showcase the development languages and frameworks used for our web and mobile applications, to assist those with a technical background in understanding our technology choices and also to showcase our tech stack to potential employees or partners.

In “Our Team”, we aim to create a personal connection between our team and visitors by showcasing a group photo of our talented members. This section is designed to emphasize the human aspect of our company, fostering trust and familiarity with our audience. Through a visually engaging group photo, we invite visitors to get to know the faces behind our work and highlight the collaborative spirit that drives our success.

Following the "Our Team" section, we featured a "Trusted By" section that highlights the brands and organizations that have placed their trust in us. This section showcases our valued partnerships and demonstrates the credibility and reputation we've earned in delivering exceptional web and mobile solutions.

Contact section

In redesigning our Contact section, we've prioritized creating an intuitive and simple user experience, offering multiple options for reaching out to us. We've replaced the complex contact form with a streamlined interface that makes it easy for visitors to connect with Xlance Collective. Whether users prefer to call, email, or visit us on social platforms, we've ensured accessibility and convenience.

Our goal is to facilitate effective communication by providing straightforward and user-friendly contact options, reflecting our commitment to customer satisfaction and responsiveness. This redesigned Contact section aims to enhance user engagement and make it effortless for visitors to connect with our team.

Working as a Wordpress Developer

I had the opportunity to collaborate closely with a skilled project manager who mentored me in developing websites using WordPress. Under his guidance and support, I successfully developed the Xlance Collective website using WordPress. This hands-on experience allowed me to refine my skills in WordPress development, including theme customization, plugin integration, and website optimization.

This experience significantly contributed to shaping my expertise with WordPress and enhancing my understanding of effective website development practices.

Result and Takeaways

Working in a startup was a challenging and enlightening experience. It taught me the importance of being agile and efficient, and how to prioritize my energy and efforts effectively.

Some key takeaways from this project are

  • Enhanced skills and understanding as a UX designer through collaboration with a project manager on a successful website redesign project.
  • Learned the importance of user-centered design and effective communication in delivering a compelling user experience.
  • Developed proficiency in wireframing, prototyping, and interface design to create intuitive and engaging interactions.
  • Emphasized empathy-driven design and iterative improvement based on user feedback to strengthen UX capabilities.
  • Acquired WordPress skills during the project, expanding technical expertise and versatility in web development.
