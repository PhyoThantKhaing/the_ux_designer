AI in Telemedicine: Connecting Patients and Doctors through Doctor Plus: Patient App


Project Overview

The Doctor Plus: Patient app is a telemedicine application powered by artificial intelligence. Users can chat or talk to an AI about their symptoms, and the AI suggests possible diseases or medical issues. The platform also includes certified doctors accessible via the Doctor Plus: Doctor app . If a user's symptoms match a doctor's specialty, the doctor can join the chat to provide further assistance, such as prescribing medication, updating medical records, or scheduling an appointment.

The Project goal is to eliminate physical waiting times in clinics and elevate telemedicine to the next level by providing immediate, AI-driven medical consultations and seamless access to healthcare professionals.

My Design Process

Background

Telemedicine has revolutionized healthcare by enabling remote medical consultations, making healthcare more accessible and convenient. However, many telemedicine solutions still face challenges such as long waiting times, inefficient communication, and limited access to specialized care.

In this The Doctor Plus: Patient app project, the goal is to create an AI-powered telemedicine platform that not only facilitates immediate medical consultations but also integrates seamlessly with real doctors to provide comprehensive healthcare services.

By allowing users to describe their symptoms through text or audio, the app uses AI to quickly analyze and suggest possible medical conditions. This immediate feedback helps users understand their health concerns and decide on the next steps. Furthermore, by connecting users with certified doctors through the Doctor Plus: Doctor app, the platform ensures that professional medical advice is always available. Doctors can review AI-generated summaries, join conversations when relevant, and offer precise medical guidance, prescriptions, and appointment scheduling.

Research findings & insights

Current Challenges in Telemedicine:

  • Patients often face long waiting times and delays in receiving medical consultations, which can lead to anxiety and potentially worsen their conditions.
  • Doctors and healthcare providers are frequently overwhelmed by the sheer volume of patients, leading to burnout and reduced quality of care.
  • Many telemedicine platforms struggle with efficiently diagnosing common health issues, resulting in misdiagnoses and unnecessary follow-ups.

Need for AI in Telemedicine:

  • AI can streamline interactions between patients and doctors, ensuring that medical professionals receive concise, relevant information and patients get clearer guidance.
  • AI algorithms can quickly analyze symptoms and provide preliminary diagnoses, significantly reducing the time patients spend waiting for medical advice.
  • By providing accurate initial assessments, AI can help patients determine when a clinic visit is necessary, thereby reducing the strain on healthcare facilities and saving time for both patients and doctors.

Understanding the problem

Many patients face significant difficulties when seeking medical care, particularly having to endure long waits at the clinic while feeling unwell, even if they have scheduled an appointment. This wait can worsen their discomfort and health. Additionally, people often avoid visiting clinics for minor illnesses, unsure whether it's necessary and unwilling to deal with the hassle. They frequently lack proper guidance on which medications to take, leading to uncertainty and potentially neglected health.

These issues highlight the urgent need for a more accessible and efficient healthcare solution that can provide immediate, trustworthy advice and alleviate the strain on both patients and healthcare providers.

Defining the MVP

Following our research, we have identified the MVP features for the app.

Symptom Input and AI Diagnosis

  • Text Input: Users can type in their symptoms.
  • Audio Input: Users can speak their symptoms, which the app transcribes and analyzes.
  • AI Analysis: The AI provides possible diagnoses and next steps based on the symptoms entered.

Doctor Matching and Consultation

  • Doctor Profiles: Display basic information about available doctors, including specialties and availability.
  • Symptom Matching: Automatically match users with doctors based on the symptoms and the doctor's specialty.
  • Request Consultation: Users can request a consultation with a matched doctor for further assistance.

Virtual Consultations

  • Chat Feature: Enable text-based consultations between users and doctors.
  • Prescription Feature: Allow doctors to prescribe medication through the app.

User Accounts and Medical Records

  • User Profiles: Basic user information and medical history.
  • Consultation History: Users can view previous consultations and diagnoses.

Design

For the The Doctor Plus: Patient app, we aimed to create a clean and minimalist UI that conveys reliable and trustworthy AI-driven medical advice.

Home

The home screen of the app exemplifies a clean and intuitive design, ensuring users can effortlessly navigate and access essential features. Key elements include a profile shortcut for quick access to user information, notifications for timely updates, and booking details for easy management of appointments. The prominent Quick CTA button facilitates seamless interaction with the reliable AI, allowing users to speak their symptoms and receive instant feedback. The layout is designed to prioritize user convenience and trust, making healthcare more accessible and efficient.

Chat

The chat screen of the app is designed to be clean and intuitive, providing users with a seamless experience. It features a search function for easy navigation, quick access to doctor's information, and a reliable voice chat option. This layout ensures that users can efficiently connect with medical professionals, making the process of seeking medical advice straightforward and trustworthy.

AI Dotor

The AI Doctor screen showcases a clean and user-friendly interface. Users can access previous AI-patient chat history, interact with an AI doctor avatar, and utilize the reliable voice chat function for more personalized assistance. This setup ensures that patients can seamlessly engage with AI-driven medical consultations, enhancing their experience and trust in the app's capabilities.

Medical History

The Medical Record screen offers users a streamlined view of their past consultations with detailed medical records written by their doctors. Users can quickly access a list of their recent medical records and tap on any entry to view comprehensive details, including diagnoses, treatment plans, and doctor's notes. This feature ensures that users have reliable and easy access to their medical history, facilitating better personal health management and communication with healthcare providers.

The Prescription screen provides users with an organized list of their previous prescriptions, written by their doctors during consultations. Users can tap on any prescription entry to view detailed information, including medication names, dosages, instructions for use, and refill details. This feature ensures that users have easy access to their medication history, enabling them to manage their prescriptions effectively and maintain a clear record for future reference or discussions with healthcare providers.

Booking an appointment

The Booking screen features two tabs: "My Appointment" and "Book Appointment". In the "My Appointment" tab, users can view a quick calendar for the week and see their upcoming appointments in a card format, allowing them to view detailed information or cancel appointments.

The "Book Appointment" tab lets users choose a healthcare specialty and view available doctors in that category. Users can check each doctor's details, including their education, experience, and client reviews, and book an appointment with their preferred doctor.

The booking process

In the booking process, users will

  • View Doctor Cards: Browse through cards of doctors.

  • Check Doctor Details: Review detailed information about the doctor.
  • Select a Package: Choose from packages provided by the doctor.

  • Select Available Schedule: Pick a convenient schedule.

  • Describe Medical Issues: Enter their medical issues or allow the app to use AI-summarized symptoms.
  • Confirm and Checkout: Confirm the details and proceed to checkout.

User profile

The profile screen includes several key sections for user convenience and accessibility:

  • Emergency Contact: Quickly access and manage emergency contact details.
  • My Profile: View and edit personal profile information.
  • Help Center: Find FAQs and contact support.
  • Privacy Policy: Review privacy terms and conditions.
  • Choose Language: Select preferred language for better accessibility.

Emergency contact

The Emergency Contact screen is designed to help users set up contacts who can be reached in case of an emergency. These contacts are typically family members, friends, or other trusted individuals who can be contacted by medical professionals or emergency responders if the user is unable to communicate during a health crisis.

Sprinting Towards Success: Our Agile Adventure

We are utilizing the Agile framework for this app to ensure an efficient project timeline and an iterative development process. Although working within the Agile framework can be challenging, it allows us to continuously refine our understanding of users' needs and enhance the overall user experience.

Some key takeaways from this project are

  • Our adoption of Agile methodology facilitated efficient project management and iterative development, ensuring timely delivery and continuous improvement.
  • Through Agile, we honed our understanding of user needs, leading to significant enhancements in user experience and functionality.
  • Usability testing and continuous refinement based on user feedback are ongoing priorities for further enhancement.
