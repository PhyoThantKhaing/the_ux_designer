AI in Telemedicine: Connecting Patients and Doctors through the Doctor App


Project Overview

The Doctor Plus: Doctor app is a telemedicine platform tailored for healthcare professionals. It enables doctors to view AI-summarized cards containing patient symptoms that match their specialty. Doctors can request to join live conversations between the patient and AI, and, once accepted, chat directly with the patient. They can assist by writing medical records, prescribing medications, and booking physical appointments.

This app aims to streamline patient-doctor interactions, enhance the telemedicine experience, and provide comprehensive medical care efficiently.

My Design Process

Background

Telemedicine has revolutionized healthcare by enabling remote medical consultations, making healthcare more accessible and convenient. However, many telemedicine solutions still face challenges such as long waiting times, inefficient communication, and limited access to specialized care.

The Doctor Plus: Doctor app is designed to work in tandem with the Doctor Plus: Patient app. When patients report their symptoms to the AI in the patient app, the AI generates summarized cards highlighting potential medical conditions. These summaries are then matched with doctors' specialties, allowing them to review relevant cases quickly.

By providing AI-summarized symptom cards, the app ensures that doctors have a clear and concise overview of the patient's condition. Doctors can then request to join the live patient-AI conversation. Upon the patient's acceptance, doctors can chat directly with the patient, offer professional advice, update medical records, prescribe medications, and even schedule physical appointments if necessary.

Research findings & insights

Current Challenges in Telemedicine:

  • Patients often experience long waiting periods and delays in receiving medical consultations, leading to increased anxiety and the potential worsening of their conditions.
  • Doctors and healthcare providers are frequently overwhelmed by the high volume of patients, which can lead to burnout and a reduction in the quality of care they can provide.
  • Many telemedicine platforms struggle with efficiently diagnosing common health issues, resulting in misdiagnoses and unnecessary follow-up appointments.

Need for AI in Telemedicine:

  • AI can facilitate more efficient interactions between patients and doctors by delivering concise and relevant information, enabling medical professionals to provide clearer guidance to patients.
  • AI algorithms can rapidly analyze symptoms and offer preliminary diagnoses, significantly reducing the waiting time for patients seeking medical advice.
  • By providing accurate initial assessments, AI helps doctor with the symptoms of patients , thereby reducing strain on healthcare facilities and saving time for both patients and doctors.

Understanding the problem

Healthcare providers today face significant challenges in delivering timely and effective care due to long patient wait times, overwhelming caseloads, and the complexities of diagnosing and treating diverse medical conditions. These issues underscore the critical need for innovative solutions that can streamline patient-doctor interactions and enhance diagnostic accuracy. The Doctor Plus: Doctor app aims to address these challenges by leveraging AI to provide doctors with summarized, AI-generated patient symptom cards that match their specialty.

This approach not only facilitates quicker assessments and informed decision-making but also improves overall healthcare efficiency and patient outcomes. By integrating AI technology with professional expertise, the app seeks to redefine telemedicine practices, ensuring that doctors can deliver personalized, high-quality care efficiently and effectively.

Personas

Defining the MVP

Following our research, we have identified the MVP features for the app.

AI-Summarized Symptom Cards

  • Automatically generated cards summarizing patient symptoms and potential conditions based on AI analysis.
  • Saves doctors time by providing concise, relevant patient information upfront.

Doctor Matching system

  • Matches patient cases with doctors based on their specialties and availability.
  • Ensures that patients are quickly connected to the appropriate specialist.

Live Chat Request Feature

  • Doctors can request to join live patient-AI conversations to provide direct medical advice.
  • Facilitates timely and efficient patient-doctor interactions.

Medical Record Management and Prescription Writing

  • Enables doctors to update patient medical records during or after consultations.
  • Allows doctors to write and send prescriptions directly through the app.

Appointment Scheduling

  • Doctors can book physical or follow-up appointments for patients within the app.
  • Simplifies the process for patients to get the care they need without additional administrative steps.

User Flow

After gathering enough information from research, personas and MVP, we created a user flow for the app.

Information Architecture

To optimize the content and structure of the Doctor Plus: Doctor app, I developed its Information Architecture. This ensures doctors can easily navigate through AI-summarized symptom cards, patient consultations, medical records management, prescription writing, and support features without any confusion.

Design

For the Doctor Plus: Doctor App, we aimed to create a clean and minimalist UI that conveys reliable and trustworthy AI-driven medical advice.

Profile set up

Profile setup process for doctors on the Doctor Plus: Doctor app. The process begins with a straightforward sign-up screen where doctors enter their basic information. Next, they complete their profile by providing details such as clinic name, phone number, location, and a brief bio. The setup also includes uploading educational documents to verify credentials.

Finally, doctors select their healthcare specialty from a comprehensive list, ensuring that the app matches them with patients whose needs align with their expertise. This streamlined setup process ensures that doctors are ready to provide telemedicine services efficiently and accurately.

Home

The home screen design ensures a seamless user experience for doctors by providing key functionalities at a glance. The profile shortcut allows quick access to the doctor's personal information and settings. AI-summarized patient info cards highlight relevant patient details, including tags for urgent issues and specific conditions. The quick call-to-action (CTA) button enables doctors to join live chats with patients instantly. Additionally, the notification icon ensures that doctors stay updated with real-time alerts, enhancing their ability to manage patient interactions efficiently.

Chat

The chat functionality of the Doctor Plus: Doctor app, designed to facilitate seamless communication between doctors and patients. The chat screen includes a search function for quickly finding previous conversations. The list of ongoing chats provides doctors with easy access to current patient interactions. Selecting a patient chat reveals AI-summarized symptoms, ensuring doctors have relevant information at a glance. The patient's quick info is prominently displayed, offering essential details at a glance. Additionally, the interface supports voice chat and file/image attachments, allowing doctors to provide comprehensive care and communicate effectively.

Live AI & Patient conversation

In Live AI and Patient Conversations feature of the Doctor Plus: Doctor app, doctors can see a list of patients with AI-summarized information about their symptoms, making it easier to prioritize and respond to urgent cases. This screen also displays detailed patient information and allows doctors to view ongoing live chats. On the live chat screen, doctors can join the conversation by reviewing AI-summarized symptoms and requesting to join the chat. This streamlined interface ensures that doctors can efficiently manage patient interactions and provide timely, informed medical advice.

Medical record

During the chat with patient, doctors can write medical records or prescription. By clicking the write icon at the top of the screen, doctors can select whether to write a medical record or a prescription for the patient. Medical record screen provides a detailed template for entering comprehensive medical records, including patient history, current medications, allergies, and more. This feature ensures that doctors can accurately document patient information and maintain thorough medical records, enhancing the quality of patient care and ensuring all critical information is readily accessible for future consultations.

Writing a prescription

During the chat with patient, doctors can write medical records or prescription. By clicking the write icon at the top of the screen, doctors can select whether to write a medical record or a prescription for the patient. Prescription screen provides a detailed template for writing a prescription, including fields for the patient's name, date of birth, and the medication to be prescribed. This feature ensures that doctors can easily and accurately prescribe medications, streamlining the treatment process and improving patient care.

Patients screen

The patient management feature in the Doctor Plus: Doctor app, the “Patient list” screen displays a list of patients that match the doctor's specialty, allowing for efficient patient sorting. The “My Appointment” screen provides a quick calendar and appointment details, enabling doctors to keep track of their schedule. The “Patient details” screen presents comprehensive patient details, including symptoms, possible diagnoses, recent medical records, prescriptions, and emergency contacts. This holistic view enhances the doctor's ability to deliver personalized and informed care, streamlining the patient management process.

Profile

The “Profile screen” provides a more detailed profile view of the doctor, including his patient count, years of experience, rating, and reviews. It also allows access to consultation fees, clinic details, and a help center. The “My profile” screen shows the profile editing interface, enabling doctors to update personal information, location, biography, educational documents, and specialty. This comprehensive profile management ensures that doctors can maintain accurate and professional profiles, enhancing their interaction with patients.

Consultation fees

In “My consultation fees” screen, doctors can add consultation packages by selecting the desired mode of communication, including messaging, video call, voice call, or in-person consultations. Doctors can also modify their consultation fees by setting or updating the charges for each package. This functionality allows doctors to easily customize and manage their consultation fees based on the type of service provided.

Clinic screen

The clinic screen displays a list of clinic staff, and selecting a member provides detailed information about their role and contact information. This organized and user-friendly interface ensures that doctors can easily manage their profiles and clinic information, enhancing the overall efficiency of their practice

Sprinting Towards Success: Our Agile Adventure

We are utilizing the Agile framework for this app to ensure an efficient project timeline and an iterative development process. Although working within the Agile framework can be challenging, it allows us to continuously refine our understanding of users' needs and enhance the overall user experience.

Some key takeaways from this project are

  • Our adoption of Agile methodology facilitated efficient project management and iterative development, ensuring timely delivery and continuous improvement.
  • Through Agile, we honed our understanding of user needs, leading to significant enhancements in user experience and functionality.
  • Usability testing and continuous refinement based on user feedback are ongoing priorities for further enhancement.

We plan to introduce features such as real-time patient health monitoring, personalized care recommendations, and seamless integration with wearable devices to further elevate the convenience and efficiency of telemedicine. Our goal is to continuously innovate and ensure that the Doctor Plus: Doctor App remains a trusted and indispensable tool for providing accessible and effective healthcare.
